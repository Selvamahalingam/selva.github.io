Workflow

How I Deliver Projects

A structured engineering process that keeps projects on-spec, on-time, and manufacturable from day one.

01

Requirement Review

Define loads, speeds, environment, standards, and project scope. Clarify all ambiguities before touching CAD.

02

Concept & Calculations

Hand-calculations or spreadsheet models to confirm feasibility. Select materials, drives, bearings, and cross-sections.

03

3D Parametric Model

Full SolidWorks parametric model with proper mates, configurations, and design intent captured throughout.

04

Review & Iterate

Client checkpoint with 3D views, section cuts, and preliminary BOM before finalizing detailed drawings.

05

Manufacturing Drawings

Complete 2D drawing package with GD&T, tolerances, weld symbols, surface finish — ready for fabrication.

06

Delivery & Support

Deliver SolidWorks files, PDFs, DXFs, and calculation reports. Post-delivery support for fabrication queries.
